The Best Series According to Vasundhara

My top 3 series of all-time.

Death Note

This is a psychological thriller that follows the story of Light Yagami, a high school student who finds a death note of a shinigami

Naruto

This is a story of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who seeks recognition from his peers and dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village.

Kuroko's Basketball

It tells the story of a high school basketball team trying to make it to the national tournament.
